About SHPE IU

Building community, advancing careers, and celebrating Hispanic excellence in STEM.

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) is a leading national organization whose mission is to empower the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.

Our chapter at Indiana University Bloomington fosters Hispanic leadership in engineering and technology through professional development, community outreach, and academic excellence — creating a familia that supports you through every step of your career.
