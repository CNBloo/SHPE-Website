About SHPE Indiana University

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) is a leading social-technical organization whose primary function is to enhance and achieve the potential of Hispanics in engineering, math, and science.

Our chapter at Indiana University, Bloomington is dedicated to fostering Hispanic leadership in STEM fields through professional development, community outreach, and academic excellence.

We provide our members with opportunities for professional growth, networking, mentorship, and community service while celebrating our rich Hispanic heritage and culture.
